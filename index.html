EPA SITE TYPES
Superfund / CERCLA
Contaminated sites on EPA's cleanup priority list. Can cloud title, restrict use, and require remediation disclosure.
RCRA Hazardous Waste
Active or former hazardous waste handlers regulated under federal law. May have ongoing compliance obligations.
TRI — Toxic Release
Facilities reporting toxic chemical releases to air, water, or land. Indicates ongoing industrial emissions, not necessarily cleanup liability.
Brownfield
Former industrial/commercial sites with known or suspected contamination. Often eligible for EPA cleanup grants — can be a redevelopment opportunity.
NPDES Wastewater
Permitted wastewater discharge point. Indicates industrial or municipal treatment operation — usually not a direct transaction risk.
VSQG — Very Small Quantity Generator
Generates ≤100 kg/month of hazardous waste. Lowest tier under RCRA — dry cleaners, auto shops, small manufacturers. Low compliance burden, rarely a transaction risk.
